Staff

Sam

Hi! I'm the (more or less anonymous) developer of Love Hues, but please feel free to call me Sam. I'm a Canadian Game Design graduate who's wanted to share my characters and stories with the world since I was little, and this game is my first step towards making that dream come true.

Love Hues has been my passion project for the last three years. As I love narrative-focused games with choices that truly matter, I played a number of them until I noticed that the casts tended to lack diversity. My friend and I then began chatting about our own visual novel, and I eventually made those plans a reality.

My biggest hope for Love Hues is that the audience will love the characters as much as I do, and that they'll feel represented in the cast I've developed. I'm always looking to learn more and I'm eager to make the game grow based on the feedback I receive!

I created Love Hues' GUI, art, writing and programming with lots of assistance of the community around me.
