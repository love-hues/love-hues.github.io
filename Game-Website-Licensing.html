Privacy Policy

Past iterations and this current iteration of Love Hues! does not transmit any kind of data from its users to Oneirocritica Games. All game data is stored locally on your device. Please note that the third party links go external websites - please review their Privacy Policy for more information.

This iteration of Love Hues! does store persistent data locally on your computer (used solely to advance the story, save unlocked gallery images, skip text and unlock new story segments), but Oneirocritica Games cannot access this in any way. If you wish to delete most of your persistent data, you can press the "Erase Persistent Data" button on the game's main menu. You can also click the 'Persistent' file in your saves folder and delete that. Please note that persistent data may also be stored elsewhere on your disk.
