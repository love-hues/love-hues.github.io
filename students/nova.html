Nova Lavallee

Quick Overview

Name: Nova Lavallee
Pronouns: she/her/hers
Age: 18
Field of Study: Bachelor of Arts (Native Studies/History)
Birthday: May 6th

"Here's my number. Since we're neighbours anyway, we might as well keep in touch, right?" Nova
​

Nova's your neighbor in the dorms, and she's often out researching Falls' strange past. She's dedicated to and protective of her friends, especially Ava. She may be more entwined in Falls' secrets than she tells most folx. (Please note: Nova's route is only unlocked after 1+ ending(s) has been achieved!)

​ She might seem a little skeptical and curt when you first meet her, but she's trusting and courageous once you grow closer. ​
