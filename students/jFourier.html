James Fourier

Quick Overview

Name: James Fourier
Pronouns: he/him/his
Age: 20
Field of Study: Bachelor of Science (Biology/Classics)
Birthday: June 17th

"But even a medical student to-be can indulge himself sometimes, right? ...Right?" James Fourier
​

Meet James! This cheery pre-med student is a third year at Falls University who has no issue befriending anyone and everyone. He's pretty flirty but he always ensures that you're OK with it. James is highly dedicated to his field and admires the work that other medical professionals have done before him.

​ If you're looking for a friend with a bright sense of humor who wants nothing more than to see everyone around him healthy and happy, be sure to chat with him! ​
