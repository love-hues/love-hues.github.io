Toby Woodley

Quick Overview

Name: Toby Woodley
Pronouns: they/them/theirs
Age: 19
Field of Study: Open Studies (Interested in Phys. Ed)
Birthday: July 22nd

"Skyler, despite my flaws and insecurities... My past..." Toby Woodley
​

Toby's full of energy and keen to kick off their studies in post-secondary - and to join Falls' soccer team! They're troubled by their past with a certain other Falls student...

Sports are their passion and they enjoy motivating people on-and-off field. Toby also works at a café and has a wealth of knowledge about sweets to share with their friends; maybe you'll also get to hear about the newest trends in confectionary making! ​
