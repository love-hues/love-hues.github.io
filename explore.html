About Love Hues

Love Hues is LGBTQ+ friendly, indie visual novel that's been in development since 2015. It is now nearing completion!

It follows your adventures as the player character (default name Skyler) as you navigate your first few months at Falls University. You'll soon meet other students who will shape your journey, but your actions will determine their very fate. Just remember that this power may have consequences...

Features

  • Love Hues has over 100,000 words and counting!
  • The game features over 27 different endings and five full routes, including one secret route!
  • Choose between 3 sets of pronouns; the five unique main characters are romanceable regardless of your choice!
  • Tired of seeing VN characters wearing the same outfits, day in and day out! Me too! So Love Hues' characters each have 3 different outfits!
  • Created entirely by one developer (with music by a composer!)
